Professor Dr. Ajay I. Dudani, M.S. (Ophth)

A prominent Vitreoretinal surgeon in practice for over two decades.

Professor Dr. Dudani is a fellowship-trained surgeon from Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai, and the National Nagoya Hospital, Japan. He further honed his skills in advanced medical retinal disease management with Professor Lawrence Yanuzzi in New York.

Key Qualifications

  • M.S. (Ophthalmology) - K.E.M. Hospital, Bombay University
  • Diplomate of National Board (Ophthalmology), New Delhi
  • Fellow of College of Physicians and Surgeons, (Ophthalmology), Bombay

Vitreoretinal Fellowships

  • Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai (under Dr. S.S. Badrinath)
  • National Nagoya Hospital, Japan (under Prof. Fumitaka Ando)
  • Retina Vitreous Macula Associates, New York (under Prof. Lawrence Yanuzzi)
  • Doheny Eye Institute, University of Southern California, LA

Professional Experience

Professor Dr. Dudani has been in private practice for many years as a Vitreoretinal Surgeon, performing complex vitreous and retinal surgeries independently. He is a Professor of Ophthalmology at K.J. Somaiya Medical College and holds honorary consultant positions at several prestigious hospitals, including Bombay Hospital and Holy Family Hospital, Bandra.

Publications & Presentations

He has published over 60 articles in peer-reviewed journals and has presented over 200 papers at national and international conferences, including the American Academy of Ophthalmology.
