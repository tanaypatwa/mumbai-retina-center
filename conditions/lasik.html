LASIK (Laser-Assisted in Situ Keratomileusis)

About LASIK

LASIK is a refractive surgery procedure intended to correct vision and reduce a person's dependency on glasses or contact lenses. It is a preferred alternative to PRK (Photo-Refractive Keratectomy) because it requires less time for full recovery and the patient experiences no pain.

Why Choose LASIK?

  • It is the preferred choice for refractive surgery by eye surgeons worldwide.
  • The procedure is done on an outpatient basis.
  • Both eyes can often be treated on the same day.
  • Experience clarity of vision within hours of surgery.
  • No more daily rituals with visual aids.

Lifestyle Benefits

  • Pursue your desired career in fields like defense services, aviation, sports, or modeling.
  • Enjoy a natural appearance without glasses.
  • Better vision for recreational activities, including water, winter, and contact sports.
  • A new visual freedom for all aspects of life.

Wavefront Guided Custom LASIK

Customized LASIK offers a higher potential for perfect vision and a lower potential for visual compromise. It is ideal for patients with complex prescriptions or those who seek the best possible visual outcome.

Using Wavefront technology, we analyze your eye's unique characteristics to create a personalized treatment plan. This approach removes any distortion from the optical path to bring objects into perfect focus, offering a greater opportunity for 20/20 vision and reducing issues like night glare.

The Treatment Process

Our professional consultants will examine your eyes using state-of-the-art equipment. To ensure accurate measurements, you will be advised to stop wearing soft contact lenses for one week and rigid lenses for two weeks before the examination.

After the Procedure

You will receive personalized aftercare instructions and eye drops. It is normal to experience some watering of the eyes, but it's important not to rub them. Follow-up appointments will be scheduled to monitor your healing and the success of the treatment.
