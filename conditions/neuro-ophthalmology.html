Neuro-Ophthalmic Conditions

About Neuro-Ophthalmic Conditions

Neuro-ophthalmology bridges neurology and ophthalmology, addressing vision problems related to the nervous system. This includes disorders of the optic nerve, visual pathways, and brain, such as optic neuritis, double vision, and vision loss from strokes or tumors. A comprehensive evaluation is crucial for diagnosing these complex conditions.
