About Oculoplastic & Orbital Disorders

This specialty focuses on the eyelids, tear ducts, and the orbit (the bony eye socket). We treat conditions like ptosis (drooping eyelids), eyelid malpositions, blocked tear ducts, orbital fractures, and tumors. This also includes cosmetic procedures to enhance the appearance of the eyes and surrounding areas.
