About Dr. Ajay Dudani

A commitment to excellence in retinal care.

A Leader in Vitreoretinal Surgery

Dr. Ajay Dudani is a distinguished ophthalmologist and one of India's leading Vitreoretinal surgeons. With over 25 years of dedicated experience, he has built a reputation for clinical excellence, pioneering research, and compassionate patient care. His mission is to provide world-class, affordable eye care and to prevent vision loss through early detection and state-of-the-art treatment.

He is a Professor at K.J. Somaiya Medical College, where he imparts his extensive knowledge to the next generation of ophthalmologists. His work is driven by a passion for innovation and a deep commitment to his patients' well-being.

World-Class Credentials & Training

Dr. Dudani's expertise is backed by an outstanding academic record and prestigious fellowships at some of the world's most respected eye institutions.

  • Fellowship: Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai, India
  • Fellowship: National Nagoya Hospital, Japan
  • Fellowship: Retina Vitreous Macula Associates, New York, USA
  • Fellowship: Doheny Eye Institute, USC Los Angeles, USA
  • Gold Medalist: D.O.M.S. Examination
  • 2nd Rank: Master of Surgery (MS) in Ophthalmology
  • Member: American Academy of Ophthalmology

Authority in Ophthalmic Research

A dedicated researcher, Dr. Dudani has made significant contributions to the field of ophthalmology with over 60 publications in peer-reviewed journals and more than 200 presentations at national and international conferences. His work continues to influence and advance the understanding and treatment of retinal diseases.
