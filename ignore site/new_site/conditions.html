Eye Conditions We Treat

Expert care for a wide spectrum of retinal and vitreous disorders.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A common complication of diabetes that affects the eyes, caused by damage to the blood vessels of the retina.

Learn More →

Retinal Detachment

An emergency situation where the retina pulls away from its normal position, which can cause vision loss if not treated quickly.

Learn More →

Macular Degeneration (AMD)

An age-related condition that causes blurring of central vision, affecting the ability to read, drive, and recognize faces.

Learn More →

Uveitis

Inflammation of the uvea, the middle layer of the eye. It can cause redness, pain, and blurred vision, and requires specialized care.

Learn More →

Cataracts

A clouding of the eye's natural lens, which lies behind the iris and the pupil, leading to decreased vision.

Learn More →

Glaucoma

A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.
