Post Operative Instructions

  • NO DIET RESTRICTION, except for DIABETICS.
  • AVOID ALL POSSIBLE INJURIES TO THE OPERATED EYE FOR TWO MONTHS. NO PLAYING WITH CHILDREN.
  • Please attend the hospital for check up and removal of dressing next day as instructed.
  • Head bath, shower, is permitted after TWO DAYS.
  • Wipe off discharge around operated eye with cotton soaked in warm water with clean hands, if necessary.
  • Minimal watering and redness of eye is usually there up to 8 weeks time.
  • All activities including cooking, kitchen activities at home are allowed.
  • Evening walk or traveling in non-crowded areas is allowed.
  • In case the vision decreases or if there is pain then report to doctor immediately.
  • Spectacle correction will be given after 8 weeks.
  • WATCHING T.V. AND READING WITH OLD GLASSES IS PERMITTED.
  • 70% of Patients may require needing later on or YAG Laser.
  • User dark glasses as per instructions.
  • Take medicines as per instructions.
